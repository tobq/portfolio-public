User Guide
Home Screen

On this screen you can open or scroll through all voice books saved on your device

Or, you can choose to record a new voice book. Note: You will need an internet connection for transcription to work, as it makes use of our cloud technologies

Transcriber

After clicking the record button, you're taking to the transcriber view

Here, you can record what ever you want and have your speed automatically transcribed.

Your mileage may vary, based on your pronunciation

Voice Books can recognise many many languages, and uses your device language as a hint for transcription

The first block of words you say are used as the title for the book

Pausing Transcription

You can pause transcription by pressing the pause button in the transcriber view

You can easily resume transcription using the record button, again

Settings

On this page you can edit the play back speed at which voice books run

You can also re-open this user guide by clicking the button at the bottom

Books are automatically saved by pressing the stop button or the back button on your phone

Viewing Book

Once a book has been recorded and saved, it can be opened from the main home page

On this screen, you start the playback of the Voice Book. This plays the original Voice Book audio, synchronised along with the highlighted words in the book's transcript

You can also pause / stop the playback at any point using the pause / stop buttons.

The books speed is decided by the playback speed setting from the settings menu

Book Options

By pressing the options button in the top right, you can open up a context menu

In this menu you can share this book (as text)
You can also listen to the original voice book audio using a player of your choice
